Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smoothie

Recipe source: Allrecipes, “Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smoothie”: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/23539/strawberry-oatmeal-breakfast-smoothie/

Ingredients

  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 14 frozen strawberries
  • 1 banana, broken into chunks
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Equipment

  • Blender
  • Measuring cup
  • Measuring spoons
  • Spoon or spatula
  • Glasses

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Add soy milk, oats, strawberries, and banana to a blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Add sugar and vanilla if desired and blend again until smooth.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
